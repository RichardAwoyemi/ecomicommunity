Ecomi Community is the first, transparent, VeVe-specific escrow service. It was built, by and for the community. We openly facilite the P2P exchange of in app currencies and collectibles for alternate cryptocurrencies.

Step 1:

Sign up, verify your email and login.

Step 2:

Purchase a transaction or create a new one - providing accurate information. (Ecomi Community will not be held liable for loss of funds caused my information inaccuracies)

Step 3:

Transfer the exact funds specified in our email within the specified timeframe (usually 24 hours).

Step 4:

Once both parties have made the transfers, we will forward the funds (minus platform & network fees) to your receiving wallet. We are increasingly automating the process but this part is manual so please allow 48 hours before getting in contact.
