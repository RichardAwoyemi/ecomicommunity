How It Works

BitClout P2P is the first really P2P exchange. To exchange, you do not need to send $BitClout to an intermediate wallet. A smart contract for BUSD is used for exchange.

BUSD is a U.S.-regulated stablecoin, fully backed by U.S. dollars: 1 BUSD = $1.00 USD.

During the exchange, the buyer sends BUSD to the smart contract address. The seller will receive them automatically after confirming the receipt of $BitClout.

Smart contracts work on the Binance Smart Chain, which gives a higher speed and lower transaction cost compared to Ethereum.

Binance is a cryptocurrency exchange that provides a platform for trading various cryptocurrencies. Binance is the largest cryptocurrency exchange in the world in terms of trading volume.

Binance Smart Chain (BSC) is a blockchain network built for running smart contract-based applications.

On the Binance exchange, BUSD can be exchanged for any other cryptocurrency or withdrawn into fiat money.
